About Madarsa Millat Islamia

Nurturing Minds, Preserving Traditions

Our History

Madarsa Millat Islamia was established in 1972 under the guidance of Maulana Hafiz Mohammad Ismail. Located in Kathaliya village of Darbhanga district in Bihar, this madarsa started with just 25 students and has now grown to become a home for over 500 students.

Our madarsa not only provides Islamic education but also focuses on the holistic development of students. Here, along with Quran, Hadith, and Fiqh, we also teach Arabic, Urdu, and English languages.

Our Vision

To prepare a generation that can maintain a balance between Islamic values and modern education.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality Islamic education and develop moral values.
